# PayrollPal Malta Security Guide

## Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

To further protect your sensitive payroll data, we strongly recommend enabling Multi-Factor Authentication.

### How to Enable MFA
1. Log in to your account.
2. Navigate to **Account Settings** -> **Security** (or go directly to `/account/security`).
3. Click on **Add Phone Number**.
4. Enter your mobile phone number in international format (e.g., `+356 79123456`).
5. Complete the reCAPTCHA verification if prompted.
6. Enter the 6-digit verification code sent to your phone via SMS.
7. Click **Verify Code**.

Once enabled, you will be required to provide an SMS verification code whenever you perform sensitive actions, such as:
- Submitting a payroll run to the Commissioner for Revenue.
- Accessing or modifying sensitive employee bank details.
- Changing company-wide security settings.

### Recovery and Backup
- If you lose access to your phone, please contact your organization's primary administrator.
- For organization admins, ensure you have your **Master Emergency Key** stored in a physical safe or secure password manager.

### Security Best Practices
- Never share your password or MFA verification codes with anyone.
- Ensure your mobile phone has a secure lock screen (PIN, Biometric).
- Review your **Audit Logs** regularly to monitor for suspicious activity.
